Our Story

How It All Began

Some love stories are written in the stars. Ours began with a chance encounter, blossomed through countless adventures, and is now leading us to the most beautiful chapter of our lives — forever together.

June 2020

The First Meeting

It was a warm summer evening at a friend's gathering. Ralph noticed Shaira's radiant smile from across the room, and from that moment, everything changed.

☕
September 2020

The First Date

A cozy dinner at a little Italian restaurant turned into a moonlit walk by the river — the night neither of them wanted to end.

🌹
February 2021

Officially Together

On Valentine's Day, surrounded by candles and roses, Ralph asked Shaira to be his girlfriend. With tears of joy, she said yes.

💕
December 2024

Travels & Adventures

From sunrise hikes to hidden beaches, Ralph and Shaira discovered the world hand in hand, filling their hearts with memories.

✈️
August 2027

The Proposal

Under a sky full of stars at their favorite lookout point, Ralph got down on one knee. Through happy tears, she whispered "Yes, a thousand times yes!"

💍
February 2, 2028

The Wedding

Our love story reaches its most magical chapter. We invite you to witness and celebrate as we say "I do" and begin our forever.

👰
"Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favorite."
— Ralph & Shaira
