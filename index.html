Our Story

How It All Began

Some love stories are written in the stars. Ours began with a chance encounter, blossomed through countless adventures, and is now leading us to the most beautiful chapter of our lives — forever together.

June 2013

The First Meeting

Ralph and Shaira are highschool classmates. It all started when she was transferred from the other section to our section. Coincidentally, When we were having a group activity, We are always on the same group. Our classmates teasing us and we started talking and laughing. We became friends and eventually fell in love.

February 2014

The Courtship

During recess time, Ralph prepared a serenade for Shaira. He sang a song with a guitar in front of her. The name of the song is "Ngiti - Ronnie Liang". After the serenade, he asked her to be his girlfriend. Unfortunately, she said no because she was not ready yet.

March 2014

Officially Together

During graduation practice, Ralph prepared a surprise for Shaira with the help of our classmates. He asked her to be his girlfriend again and this time, she said yes. We don't know if this is coincidence or fate, but the time that she said yes is the same number of our year and section 4:17PM (4th year, section 17)

March 2025

The Proposal

At the beach where they they were celebrating their anniversary, Ralph planned a surprise proposal. Ralph got down on one knee. Through happy tears, she said "Yes!"

February 2, 2028

The Wedding

Our love story reaches its most magical chapter. We invite you to witness and celebrate as we say "I do" and begin our forever.

"Every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favorite."
— Ralph & Shaira
